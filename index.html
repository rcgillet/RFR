About

The Amazing Race for Rescue presented by Catholic Federal Credit Union is a fun exciting road rally that will raise awareness and funds for the Good Samaritan Rescue Mission. Teams of four will experience the thrill of deciphering clues and completing tasks as they make their way though Bay City, discovering what great services and products our charitable businesses offer our community. The event will end with the Finish Line Festivities, celebrating those businesses and teams who worked together to help the homeless right here in Bay County, we will eat, award prizes and have a silent auction.

How To Play

When:

Saturday, September 21st, 2013

Cost:

$160 per team

Teams:

4 players per team (must be over 18 years old to play)

Prizes:

Teams receive 4 player t-shirts, 4 dinner tickets, and the chance to win $1,000!

Register:

Register at CFCU. Please bring payment, a team name, each player's name, and contact information for the team captain

Each team must have a car to play, the game begins at 2pm and takes approximately 3-5 hours to complete. Clues send you to sponsors where you must complete a task before receiving your next clue. The first team to complete all the tasks and arrive at the Finish Line Festivities win the Grand Prize of $1,000! Silent Auction and Dinner catered by the Bay City Country Club begins at 7pm at the Prime Event Center, tickets are $25.

This event benefits the Good Samaritan Rescue Mission which provides a place of care for single men, women, mothers with children, and intact families. All clients receive three meals a day, a warm bed, a safe environment, free laundry facilities, clothing, hygiene products, and staff advocacy to assist in solving their homelessness. The goal of Mission staff is to demonstrate Christian care by providing a hand up out of trouble, not a hand out.
